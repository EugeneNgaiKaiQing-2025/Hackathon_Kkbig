MINISTRY OF HEALTH MALAYSIA - REFERRAL GUIDELINES 2025

1. BRAIN TUMORS / NEUROLOGY
- Urgent Referral Criteria: Mass effect > 5mm, midline shift, or acute neurological deficit.
- Designated Referral Centers:
  * Hospital Kuala Lumpur (HKL) - Department of Neurosurgery.
  * Hospital Sultanah Aminah (Johor Bahru).
  * Hospital Umum Sarawak (Kuching).
- SOP: Stabilize patient, administer Dexamethasone 4mg IV if edema is present, and arrange ambulance transfer.

2. LUNG MASSES / ONCOLOGY
- Suspicious nodules > 1cm require biopsy.
- Referral Center: Institut Kanser Negara (IKN) or nearest General Hospital Respiratory Unit.
- SOP: Order contrast CT Thorax/Abdomen/Pelvis for staging before referral.

3. BONE FRACTURES (ORTHOPEDICS)
- Open fractures: Emergency surgery required within 6 hours.
- Referral: Nearest district hospital with Orthopedic specialist.
- Communited/Displaced fractures: Require internal fixation (ORIF).

4. GENERAL ENQUIRIES
- For unknown cases, consult the State Health Department (JKN) hotline: 03-88810200.
